💡 Quick Cooking Tips

Season As You Go

Build layers of flavor by seasoning at every stage of cooking, not just at the end.

Mise En Place

Prepare and measure all ingredients before you start cooking for a smoother experience.

Fresh Is Best

Use fresh, seasonal ingredients whenever possible. They need less effort to taste amazing.

Sharp Knives Matter

A sharp knife is safer and more efficient. Invest in a good chef's knife and keep it honed.
