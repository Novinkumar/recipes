💡 Meal Planning Tips

1
📝

Plan Ahead

Set aside 15 minutes each weekend to plan the upcoming week's meals. It saves time and reduces stress.

2
🔄

Repurpose Leftovers

Cook once, eat twice! Transform Monday's roast chicken into Tuesday's chicken salad wraps.

3
🌈

Eat the Rainbow

Include a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables throughout the week for balanced nutrition.

4
📦

Batch Cook

Prepare grains, proteins, and sauces in bulk on weekends for quick assembly during busy weekdays.
