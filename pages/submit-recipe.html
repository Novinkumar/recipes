📝 Submission Guidelines

✅ What Makes a Great Recipe Submission

  • Clear, descriptive title that tells people what the dish is
  • Well-written description explaining what makes the recipe special
  • Precise ingredient measurements (cups, tablespoons, grams)
  • Step-by-step instructions that are easy to follow
  • A high-quality photo of the finished dish
  • Accurate prep time, cook time, and serving information
  • Helpful tips and possible substitutions

❌ Please Avoid

  • Copying recipes from other websites or cookbooks
  • Using stock photos instead of your own dish photos
  • Vague measurements like "some" or "a bit"
  • Incomplete instructions that skip important steps
  • Promotional content or spam
