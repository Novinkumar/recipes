🛍️ Smart Shopping Tips

1
📋

Organize by Aisle

Group items by store section — produce, dairy, meat, pantry — so you don't backtrack through the store.

2
🏷️

Check Sales First

Browse weekly flyers before shopping. Adjust your meal plan to take advantage of what's on sale.

3
🥫

Check Your Pantry

Before heading out, scan your fridge and pantry. Remove items you already have from the list.

4
🥦

Buy Seasonal

Seasonal produce is fresher, tastier, and often cheaper. Ask your grocer what's in season.
