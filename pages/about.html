Making cooking accessible, enjoyable, and delicious for everyone.

📖 Our Story

How a passion for home cooking became a global community.

RecipeHub was born in 2023 from a simple idea: cooking at home should be easy, fun, and stress-free. Our founders, a group of passionate home cooks and tech enthusiasts, noticed that finding reliable recipes, planning weekly meals, and managing grocery lists involved juggling multiple apps, bookmarks, and scribbled notes.

We set out to build a single, beautifully designed platform that brings everything together — recipe discovery, meal planning, shopping lists, and a vibrant community of food lovers who share their creations.

Today, RecipeHub serves over 50,000 home cooks worldwide, with a curated library of 2,500+ tested recipes spanning every cuisine, dietary preference, and skill level. Whether you're a college student making your first stir-fry or a seasoned home chef perfecting your sourdough, RecipeHub has something for you.

Our mission is simple: to empower everyone to cook with confidence and joy.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RecipeHub.

Is RecipeHub free to use? +
Yes! RecipeHub is completely free to use. You can browse recipes, save favorites, plan meals, and create shopping lists without any subscription or payment. We believe great cooking should be accessible to everyone.
How can I submit my own recipe? +
Simply navigate to the Submit Recipe page. Fill in the recipe details including title, ingredients, instructions, and a photo. Our team reviews submissions within 48 hours before publishing them to the community.
Are the recipes tested before publishing? +
Absolutely. Every recipe in our curated collection has been tested by our culinary team for accuracy, taste, and clarity. Community-submitted recipes are reviewed for completeness and flagged if there are any issues. Our star rating system also helps surface the best recipes.
Can I use RecipeHub on my phone? +
Yes! RecipeHub is designed mobile-first, meaning it works beautifully on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. No app download is needed — just visit the website in your mobile browser. You can even add it to your home screen for quick access.
How does the meal planner work? +
The Meal Planner lets you assign recipes to breakfast, lunch, and dinner for each day of the week. Simply tap an empty slot, choose a recipe, and it's added. You can then generate a shopping list from your meal plan with one tap. All data is saved locally on your device.
Is my data private and secure? +
Your data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information on our servers. You can export or delete your data at any time from the Profile settings page.
Can I filter recipes by dietary preference? +
Yes! You can filter recipes by vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, keto, dairy-free, and more. Use the category filters on the Recipes page or the advanced filters on the Search page to find recipes that match your dietary needs.
How do I contact the RecipeHub team? +
You can reach us through the contact form below, email us at hello@recipehub.com, or connect with us on social media. We typically respond within 24 hours on business days. We love hearing from our community!
